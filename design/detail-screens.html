Namma Metro Purple Line Extension Opens Next Month
5 Apr 2026
3 min read

The much-awaited Purple Line extension of the Namma Metro is set to begin operations next month, connecting Whitefield to Challaghatta. The Bangalore Metro Rail Corporation Limited (BMRCL) confirmed that trial runs have been completed successfully.

The extension adds 8 new stations spanning 13.5 kilometres, significantly improving connectivity for the IT corridor. Peak-hour frequency will be every 4 minutes, with the first train departing at 5:30 AM.

Commuters travelling from Whitefield to Majestic can expect the journey to take approximately 35 minutes, compared to the current 90-minute bus ride during peak traffic. Monthly passes will be available at ₹1,400.

BBMP Announces Property Tax Rebate for Early Payments
5 Apr 2026
2 min read

Bruhat Bengaluru Mahanagara Palike has announced a 5% rebate on property tax for residents who complete their payments before April 30th. The move is aimed at encouraging early compliance and improving the corporation's revenue collection.

The rebate applies to both residential and commercial properties across all eight BBMP zones. Payments can be made online through the BBMP Sakala portal or at designated ward offices. Digital payments via UPI will also be accepted.

Last year, early payment compliance stood at 38%. The corporation hopes this incentive will push the figure above 55% this financial year. Property owners with pending arrears from previous years will need to clear those first before being eligible for the current year's rebate.
